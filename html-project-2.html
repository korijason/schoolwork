Available delicacies include:

Appetizers

  • Bruschetta:
    A classic Italian appetizer consisting of toasted bread slices topped with a mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. It can include additional toppings like mozzarella cheese or prosciutto.

  • Stuffed Mushrooms:
    Mushroom caps filled with a savory mixture typically made from breadcrumbs, garlic, Parmesan cheese, herbs (such as parsley or thyme), and sausage or bacon. They are baked until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.

  • Caprese Salad:
    A simple Italian salad made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. The ingredients are layered or arranged on a plate, with slices of tomato and mozzarella alternating, and then garnished with fresh basil leaves. It's often seasoned with salt and pepper and drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

  • Spinach and Artichoke Dip:
    A creamy and flavorful appetizer made with spinach, artichoke hearts, cream cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, garlic, and various cheeses (such as Parmesan and mozzarella). It's typically baked until bubbly and served hot with tortilla chips, bread slices, or vegetable sticks for dipping.

    • Main Courses

    • Grilled Salmon with Lemon Butter Sauce:
      Grilled salmon fillet seasoned with herbs and served with a tangy lemon butter sauce. The salmon is typically cooked to perfection with a crispy exterior while remaining tender and flaky inside.

    • Pasta Carbonara:
      An Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti or other pasta shapes, tossed with a creamy sauce of eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta (or bacon), and black pepper. The heat from the pasta cooks the egg mixture, creating a rich and velvety sauce.

    • Steak Frites:
      A classic French dish consisting of a grilled or pan-seared steak (commonly a cut like sirloin or ribeye) served with crispy French fries. It's often accompanied by a side of sauce such as béarnaise or peppercorn sauce.

    • Chicken Marsala:
      An Italian-American dish featuring chicken breasts pounded thin and sautéed until golden brown, then simmered in a Marsala wine sauce with mushrooms and sometimes garlic and herbs. The sauce is rich, savory, and slightly sweet from the Marsala wine.

    • Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu:
      A vegetable stir-fry dish featuring a colorful array of vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas) stir-fried with tofu in a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil. It's often served over steamed rice or noodles.

    Desserts

    • Molten Chocolate Lava Cake:
      A decadent dessert featuring a rich chocolate cake with a gooey, molten chocolate center. It's typically served warm and often accompanied by vanilla ice cream or whipped cream. The contrast between the warm, flowing chocolate center and the cake's texture makes it a favorite among chocolate lovers.

    • Tiramisu:
      An Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers (savoiardi biscuits) and mascarpone cheese. It's flavored with cocoa powder and sometimes coffee liqueur, creating a creamy and indulgent dessert with a hint of coffee bitterness.

    • Crème Brûlée:
      A classic French dessert consisting of a rich custard base (usually flavored with vanilla) topped with a layer of caramelized sugar. The sugar is caramelized using a culinary torch to create a crisp, caramelized crust that contrasts with the smooth, creamy custard underneath.
